IEEE

IEEE stands for the Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers, a technical professional organization that promotes the development of technology and innovation. IEEE is the world's largest technical professional organization and is made up of engineers, scientists, and other professionals.

IEEE's mission includes:

PROMOTING TECHNOLOGY

Advancing the development and application of electrotechnology and related sciences.

IMPROVING STANDARDS

Fostering the development of standards that can become national and international standards.

PUBLISHING

Publishing journals, magazines, conference proceedings, and standards

HONOURING ACHIEVERS

Sponsoring programs to recognize individuals, colleges, and leaders who have made a positive impact on their profession and technology.

IEEE's headquarters are in New York, USA.
